When the alpha is zero, the background is totally transparent: rgba(255, 70, 190, 0)

The opacity slightly decreases when you increase the value of alpha: rgba(255, 70, 190, 0.2)

This is more opaque but still lighter: rgba(25, 70, 190, 0.4)

Keep increasing the value and you will see darker background without changing the colors: rgba(255, 70, 190, 0.6)

Getting dense: rgba(255, 70, 190, 0.8)

This is the densest of all: rgba(255, 70, 190, 1)

Value 1 for alpha is the maximum value. You cannot increase it any further. Even if you do, it has no effect on the transparency of the background: rgba(255, 70 ,190, 2)

This is the most professional method to manipulate colors without changing the original values
